How the ritual works

A deck, a question, a quote, and a short audio reflection. Simple enough to repeat often. Deep enough to matter.

Step 1

Draw

Pull a card at random. Don’t hunt for the “right” one. Let the question arrive as it is.

Step 2

Sit with it

Read the question slowly. Notice which words feel heavy, sharp, or unexpectedly soft.

Step 3

Listen

Scan the QR to hear the AI conversation unpack the card with you—a quiet “echo” of the prompt.

Step 4

Respond

Journal, think, walk, or speak it out loud. The point is not a perfect answer, but an honest one.

The Philosophy

⚪ White Cards

“Who are you?” These cards invite introspection, exploring identity, emotional honesty, and inner truth.

⚫ Black Cards

“Where are you going?” These cards explore vision, ambition, courage, and creative risk.

“The goal is not to provide answers. The goal is to give people back to themselves—through better questions.”

For individuals

Morning rituals, night-time reflections, solo walks, and quiet journaling sessions where you want a deeper prompt than “How was your day?”

For duos & circles

Use a single card to open a deeper conversation with a partner, friend, or small group—one question at a time.

For teams

Swap icebreakers for real breakers. Start strategy sessions, retros, or offsites with one shared card and five minutes of honest thinking.
